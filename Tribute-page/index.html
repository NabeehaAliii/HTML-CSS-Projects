Dr. Israr Ahmed

A man who changed a million lives

Here's a time line of Dr. Israr Ahmed life:

  • 1932 - Dr. Israr Ahmed born on April 26 in Haryana, British India (now in Pakistan).

  • 1967 - Founded Tanzeem-e-Islami, a religious and educational organization.

  • 1971 - Started the radio program "Al-Hashr" to teach the Quran.

  • 1981- Established Markazi Anjuman Khuddam-ul-Quran in Lahore.

  • 1982 - Published renowned Tafseer (Quranic exegesis).

  • 2001 - Received the Sitara-i-Imtiaz civil award in Pakistan.

  • 2008- Initiated the "Quran Academy" for Quranic education.

  • 2010 Dr. Israr Ahmed passed away on April 14 in Lahore, Pakistan.
